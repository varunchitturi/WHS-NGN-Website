NSMG Training Modules (only for team participants)

The NGN Stock Market Game (NSMG) is powered by the SIFMA Foundation. Team participants learn to invest in a virtual equity portfolio (typically 100,000 US dollars) consisting of stocks, mutual funds, and bonds. Unlike other clubs, NSMG investing is based on three central factors: Environmental, Social, and Corporate Governance (ESG). You will have to invest and build your portfolio over the months. The formula to win is simply for you to gain the most amount of money with a focus on investing in ESG. Be careful with the companies you invest in. Throughout the year, teams will build their portfolio and quarterly reports to display at our final competition called NGCC. There will be a virtual event in January where you would present your reports. Through presentations, competitors will show their investment report and dive into their experience as investors.
